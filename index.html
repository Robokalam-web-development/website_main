About

ROBOKALAM TECHNOLOGIES ( Literature of Robotics ) is a robotics end to end and eminent training provider company, which focuses on education solutions, covering Robotics Education and doing Research & development in Robotics, Quad-copter etc. We provide Workshops, Training Certifications, DIY (Do It Yourself) Kits to institutes all across South India via a good delivery network. The current robotics training system provides a hands-on experience and exposure in the latest technologies to the students. We organized district level and state level championships. Apart from this, we have organized Outreach Programs in Government Institutions. Every Year, we used to organize our own well reputed fest named SKOOL ENGINE in association Samaritans Stand Up Organisation. We have been successfully organizing the annual editions of SKOOL ENGINE since 2015.

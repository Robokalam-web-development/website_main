About Us


Who Are We?

ROBOKALAM TECHNOLOGIES ( Literature of Robotics ) is a robotics end to end and eminent training provider company, which focuses on education solutions, covering Robotics Education and doing Research & development in Robotics, Quad-copter etc. We provide Workshops, Training Certifications, DIY (Do It Yourself) Kits to institutes all across South India via a good delivery network. The current robotics training system provides a hands-on experience and exposure in the latest technologies to the students. We organized district level and state level championships. Apart from this, we have organized Outreach Programs in Government Institutions. Every Year, we used to organize our own well reputed fest named SKOOL ENGINE in association Samaritans Stand Up Organisation. We have been successfully organizing the annual editions of SKOOL ENGINE since 2015.








Vision

We want to act as a mediator between Technology & Students to improve their academic performance by providing additional syllabus inputs and Best Robotics Training, expert curriculum and various Emerging Technologies. Our vision is to empower young minds through high quality & endless education.



Mission

Our main motive is to promote the experiential education among Indian children by organizing Workshops, Training and Competitions at State and National Level. We saw the enthusiasms among the students of doing something creative so we planed to provide them a National Level Platform to perform and to show their creativity. The workshops would help students to enter in the real world of Robotics & Technology and to create an aptitude in the limitless field of these emerging technologies. The workshops will guide them to get the feel of Robotics & Technology and how powerful and exciting it can be…
